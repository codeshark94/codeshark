# Code of Conduct

Codex-codeshark welcomes respectful, technically focused participation.

## Expected behavior

- Be clear, constructive, and considerate.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy and never publish another person's credentials or personal data.
- Give maintainers time to investigate and respond.
- Keep reports relevant to the project's intentionally narrow scope.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberately submitting secrets, malware, or unsafe proof-of-concept payloads to public threads
- Repeated spam, disruption, or bad-faith engagement

## Enforcement

The repository owner may edit, hide, lock, or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may restrict participation when necessary. Security vulnerabilities should be reported privately according to [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
